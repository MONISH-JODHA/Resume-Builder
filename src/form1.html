A resume is a concise document that summarizes your education, work experience, skills, and achievements. It is typically used when applying for jobs or internships to showcase your qualifications and suitability for a particular position.

The purpose of a resume is to provide a snapshot of your relevant qualifications, allowing employers to quickly assess your suitability for a job. It typically includes sections such as:

-: Name
-: Contact Information
-: Objective or Summarry
-: Education
-: Work Experience
-: Projects
-: Skills
